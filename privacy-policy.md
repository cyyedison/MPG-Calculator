# Privacy Policy — MPG Calculator

*Last updated: March 2026*

## Overview

MPG Calculator is a fuel economy tracking app for Android. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

**MPG Calculator does not collect any personal data.**

The app does not:
- Require an account or sign-in
- Connect to the internet
- Send any data to external servers
- Use advertising or analytics SDKs
- Track your location

## Data Storage

All data you enter (odometer readings, fuel amounts, car names, and settings) is stored **locally on your device** using Android's built-in database (Room/SQLite). This data never leaves your device.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:
- Deleting individual records within the app
- Using the delete all button within the app
- Uninstalling the app, which removes all associated data

## Third-Party Services

MPG Calculator does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be published at this URL with a revised date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/eddiec/MPGCalculator).
